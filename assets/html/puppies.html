Puppies

Puppies are $2,800 and are sold on a spay/neuter contract. Please see here for information about puppy buying etiquette.

All puppies are raised indoors from the moment they are born to the moment they go home with their new families. By raising my puppies indoors, they will be familiar with the sounds, smells, and sights of a home which will set your puppy up for success.

I place my puppies into homes where they are going to live in the house and be part of the family. Whether they are being placed as a family companion or as a potential show dog, my goal is to breed the temperament and character that is the hallmark of this breed: gentle, friendly, and easygoing. I strive to produce a healthy and structurally correct puppy with correct “Golden Temperament”.

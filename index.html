Welcome!

Welcome, my name is Margie Sarkin, and after living in South Florida for 35 years, I relocated to Georgia in 2006. I am located in the beautiful, Lake Oconee Region, about 1 hour east of Atlanta. My family and I have owned and loved Goldens for over 35 years.

I became involved in AKC competition in 1997. I have competed in all AKC venues, and titled multiple Golden Retrievers in Obedience, Tracking, Hunt tests, Agility, and AKC Bench Champions.

I served as the President of Everglades GRC from 2003-2004 and as Tracking Director for OTCPBC in 2005. I am currently serving as Vice President for the Atlanta Golden Retriever Club. My goals are to continue educating myself and others about the breed so close to my heart. I strive to produce companions that exemplify correct golden temperament, soundness, and good health.

I have a small, select breeding program with litters available occasionally. Of special interest to me is producing multi-purpose Goldens that are as comfortable snuggled in bed as they are in any venue you could want to explore, as well as being beautiful representatives of the breed. All Goldens used in my breeding program have OFA hip & elbow clearances, CERF (eye exams) annually, and hearts cleared by Board certified cardiologists.
